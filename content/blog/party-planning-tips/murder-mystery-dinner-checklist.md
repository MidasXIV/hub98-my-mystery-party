## One Week Before

- Confirm guest count and dietary notes.
- Assign character briefs.
- Pick background music and decor.

## Day Of

- Prep appetizers, set up drinks.
- Print or bookmark the case briefing.
- Open the Objectives panel and test the platform on your device.

## During the Dinner

- Start with intros + briefing.
- Serve mains during evidence exploration.
- Wrap with a light dessert and final reveal.

## Quick Prompts to Use

- "Summarize key contradictions so far."
- "Compare the June 10 diary entry with the chat log."
- "List the steps to verify the diner receipt alibi."
