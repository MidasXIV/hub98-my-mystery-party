## Outdoor-Friendly Setups

- Shade first: canopies, umbrellas, porch cover.
- Seating mix: picnic blankets + chairs.
- Bluetooth speaker and simple ambient playlist.

## Easy Summer Menu

- Cool apps: fruit skewers, veggie trays, dips.
- Hydration station: iced tea, lemon water, mocktails.
- Keep it portable and mess-free.

## Light Mysteries Work Best

Choose cases with quick beats and clear objectives. Use our platform’s natural language tools to summarize and move fast.

## Photo Ops & Props

- Polaroid or phone stands.
- Themed sunglasses or detective badges.
- A "case solved" banner for final pics.

## Heat Management Tips

- Schedule late afternoon or evening.
- Provide bug spray and sunscreen.
- Short breaks between acts.
