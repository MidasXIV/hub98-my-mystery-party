## The Classics

- Trench coat + notebook
- Noir glam (dark suit or dress, bold lip)

## Retro Vibes

- 80s neon sleuth
- Y2K hacker chic

## Cozy Whodunit

- Knit sweater, round glasses, satchel

## Statement Accessories

- Detective badge, magnifier, fedora
- Clip-on mic for faux interrogation

## Quick Tips

- Comfort first—you’ll move around.
- Choose one bold accessory.
- Coordinate as a group for photos.
