Looking sharp matters. Here are ten crowd‑pleasing costumes—each with a quick prop idea—to elevate your next mystery night.

## 1) The Noir Detective
Trench coat, fedora, notebook. Prop: matchbook from a shady bar.

## 2) The Femme Fatale
Silk gloves, bold lipstick, jewel pendant. Prop: hidden note in a compact.

## 3) The Mastermind
Tailored suit, minimal accessories. Prop: coded ledger pages.

## 4) The Archivist
Cardigan, glasses, vintage files. Prop: catalog card with a clue.

## 5) The Cat Burglar
Black ensemble, agile flair. Prop: velvet pouch with replica gems.

## 6) The Journalist
Press badge, messenger bag. Prop: polaroids with timestamps.

## 7) The Heir Apparent
Statement ring, heirloom cameo. Prop: will draft with missing page.

## 8) The Forensic Analyst
Lab coat, blue nitrile gloves. Prop: mock swab + report.

## 9) The Librarian‑Detective
Pleated skirt, brooch, pen. Prop: marginalia in rare book.

## 10) The Gentleman Thief
Cane, pocket square, charm. Prop: engraved calling card.

Pair your look with a character brief and you’re golden.
 
> Pro tip: A small prop makes roleplay more natural.
 
### Quick props checklist
- Calling card
- Pocket notebook
- Polaroids
- Velvet pouch
